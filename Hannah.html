I’ve never really felt unsafe

  • Home
  • Hannah

Hannah’s story began

over a year ago when she took a GIS internship working in Zaatari refugee camp for an international NGO. Her job description includes mapping camp infrastructure and conducting needs assessments within the camp. The day of our interview Hannah had piloted a participatory mapping project to assess perceived safety in the camp. Our interview focused on the camp itself, borders in- and outside the camp, and experiences of safety.
